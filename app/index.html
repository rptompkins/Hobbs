Strategy Design, Inc. collaborates with business leaders to help identify and update the goals and outcomes they want to achieve.

When customized leadership training and coaching is one of the strategies to achieve goals, Strategy Design Inc. provides the training and facilitation needed to make the vision a reality, both within the company four walls and within the marketplace.

Strategy

Lynne identifies strategic goals by working with team leaders to determine the desired outcomes of meetings, interviews, agendas, surveys, and assessments, and the actions necessary for achieving each step toward the goals.

Leadership

After understanding a team's leadership goals, Lynne will work with managers to build cohesive and successful teams. She is one of the group to become a Facilitator of The Five Behaviors of a Cohesive Team based on Patrick Lencioni's research and books."

Tools

We use various tools, when appropriate, to enhance client applications. Different versions of Everything DiSC Profiles are used depending on the desired outcomes."

Lynne Hobbs, Founder

Lynne Hobbs has 30 years of experience as a Strategic Consultant, Facilitator, and Coach. She works with clients to define key goals, both short and long term, including the professional and leadership skills necessary to achieve those goals. Collaborative and engaging, her clients find Lynne's expertise successful facilitating diverse groups they can commit to and take action to achieve.

When not leading teams, Lynne is an enthusiastic hiker, kayaker, family member, and friend.

“I use the Everything DiSC profiles for medical students, faculty and staff in the Primary Care Leadership Track at Duke. It is part of their leadership training. It is exciting to see how quickly the group catches on to the benefit of knowing their style and the style of others on the team. It gives the group confidence that they each have something special to add and that they don't have to judge each other but look for the gifts each style offers. It breaks down barriers and enchances understanding faster than anything I have seen before for teams!”

- Barbara Sheline, MD, MPH
