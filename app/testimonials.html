Results and Testimonials:

Team Building...

Work group team members not only gain an understanding of individual differences, they also come to see that results improve dramatically by valuing those different perspectives. They learn how to:

  • adapt communication to get better results
  • work more efficiently with each other
  • gain influence and improve working relationships with those most different in style
  • build trust, work through conflict, and hold each other accountable

Lynne has a unique ability. She was able to step in to a complex organization, quickly gain a solid understanding of the dynamics of the management team and help everyone on that team understand how they worked together. She then provided realistic tools and advice that helped us be even better at what we do.

Fire Chief Steve Marler, San Juan County Fire Department

Leadership Development...

A manufacturing company transitioning from non-union to union realized that its supervisory staff needed leadership and team development skills. Over several months, the group met for half days in leadership training facilitated by Lynne that combined training and actionable strategies for improving the team culture.

The results were immediately positive, including a realization that the leadership team could deliver measurable improvements in employee retention, uptime, safety and production.

Lynne Hobbs has worked with Simpson Lumber management teams at several of our manufacturing facilities over the last four years. Lynne is a skilled trainer and facilitator. She did an excellent job helping us develop and implement an extended leadership development process with very positive results.

Jack McGuill, Vice President Human Resources, Simpson Lumber Company

Strategic Action Planning, Executive/Board Retreats:

A nationally-recognized emergency medical service has brought Lynne back again to design and facilitate another retreat for the entire organization.

This highly complex and dedicated group of diverse personalities, specialties, and roles participated in planning for the emerging needs of the community, all driven by their Mission to provide excellent patient care.

Lynne is not only an exceptional facilitator, she is an exceptional person. Her care and supportive attitude helped our agency move from “difficult time” to “We’re in this together”. A feat, believe me. I highly recommend her.

Cady Chapman Davies, San Juan Island EMS, Executive Assistant/PIO Officer, SJC Public Hospital Dist #1 Records Officer

Coaching:

Lynne coached a group of non-profit managers in their effort to increase applications for the self-help housing they develop.

I have known Lynne for 25 years and she has coached me around successfully using several DiSC products and activities (including Everything DiSC and Work of Leaders) with a wide array of teams, including those in large, national organizations; those in smaller, regionals organizations. Her expert guidance is always precisely right on the money.

Ellyn McKay, CEO Vision, LLC
